Academics at Shanti Global Academy

Laying Strong Foundations for Lifelong Learning

Our Academic Programs

Comprehensive education programs designed to nurture young minds from their earliest years through primary education

Nursery

Our nursery program focuses on early emotional and sensory development through carefully crafted play-based learning experiences.

  • Emotional development through guided play
  • Sensory exploration activities
  • Basic social skills development
  • Language immersion through songs and stories
  • Art and craft for motor skill development
  • Safe and nurturing environment

Kindergarten (KG)

Introduction to formal learning with focus on literacy, numeracy, fine motor skills, and establishing healthy classroom habits.

  • Phonics-based reading introduction
  • Number recognition and basic math concepts
  • Fine motor skill development
  • Structured classroom routines
  • Creative expression through art
  • Interactive learning through games

Prep Class

Academic readiness program with structured learning approach, emphasizing creativity through hands-on projects and activities.

  • Reading fluency and comprehension
  • Mathematical problem-solving skills
  • Science exploration and discovery
  • Project-based learning approach
  • Creative writing and expression
  • Leadership and teamwork skills

Class 1

Foundation building with focus on basic literacy, numeracy, and introducing formal academic subjects in an engaging manner.

  • English language fundamentals
  • Mathematics - numbers and operations
  • Environmental Studies introduction
  • Hindi language basics
  • Art and craft activities
  • Physical education and sports

Class 2

Strengthening foundational skills with enhanced reading, writing, and basic mathematical concepts through interactive learning.

  • Advanced reading and writing skills
  • Addition and subtraction mastery
  • Science and nature exploration
  • Social studies introduction
  • Creative arts and music
  • Computer basics introduction

Class 3

Expanding academic horizons with comprehensive subjects and developing critical thinking through hands-on activities.

  • Grammar and vocabulary expansion
  • Multiplication and division concepts
  • Environmental science projects
  • Indian culture and traditions
  • Drawing and painting techniques
  • Basic computer skills

Class 4

Building comprehensive knowledge base with structured learning across all subjects and encouraging independent thinking.

  • Advanced English comprehension
  • Fractions and decimal introduction
  • Science experiments and observations
  • Geography and history basics
  • Creative writing skills
  • Technology integration

Class 5

Transitioning to middle school concepts with emphasis on analytical thinking and comprehensive subject knowledge.

  • Literature and poetry appreciation
  • Advanced mathematical concepts
  • Scientific method introduction
  • Indian history and civics
  • Public speaking and presentation
  • Digital literacy skills

Class 6

Middle school foundation with specialized subjects and developing research skills for comprehensive learning.

  • English literature and grammar
  • Algebra and geometry basics
  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology introduction
  • World geography and ancient history
  • Sanskrit or third language option
  • Project-based learning approach

Class 7

Advancing academic rigor with specialized science subjects and developing analytical and research capabilities.

  • Advanced English communication
  • Pre-algebra and coordinate geometry
  • Life processes and ecosystems
  • Medieval history and civics
  • Computer programming basics
  • Creative arts and music theory

Class 8

Pre-high school preparation with comprehensive subject knowledge and critical thinking development.

  • Literature analysis and creative writing
  • Linear equations and mensuration
  • Chemical reactions and force & motion
  • Modern history and constitution
  • Advanced computer applications
  • Leadership and teamwork skills

Class 9

High school foundation with CBSE curriculum focus and preparation for board examinations ahead.

  • English literature and language skills
  • Algebra, geometry, and statistics
  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology in depth
  • Social Science - History, Geography, Civics
  • Hindi literature and language
  • Board exam preparation techniques

Class 10

Board examination preparation with comprehensive review, practice tests, and career guidance for future academic choices.

  • Board exam focused English preparation
  • Advanced mathematics and applications
  • Science practicals and theory mastery
  • Social Science comprehensive study
  • Mock tests and exam strategies
  • Career counseling and guidance

Comprehensive education programs from Nursery to Class 10, designed to nurture young minds and prepare them for future academic success
